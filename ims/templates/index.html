Revolutionize the Way You Manage Inventory with Mazi Inventory

Streamline your stock, boost efficiency, and unlock growth with our powerful inventory management software.

Real Time Inventory Tracking

Monitor your inventory levels with real-time updates. Our software ensures that every item, across multiple locations or sales channels, is tracked down to the last unit.

Warehouse Management

Manage every aspect of your warehouse with advanced tools for tracking inventory location, managing stock levels, and overseeing order fulfillment.

Reporting & Analysis

Get a full breakdown of your inventory activity through custom reports. Track key metrics such as stock turnover, shrinkage rates, and sales trends.

Mobile Access

Stay in control of your stock whether you're in the office, on the road, or on the warehouse floor – in real time from any device.

Automated Stock Replenishment

Set minimum stock levels and let our system auto-generate purchase orders when inventory drops below thresholds.

Multi-Channel Management

Whether you're selling online, in-store, or through a distribution network, manage everything from one centralized dashboard.

Solutions

Tailored Inventory Solutions for Every Industry

Whether you're in retail, manufacturing, or e-commerce, our software is built to solve your unique challenges.

RETAIL
Optimize your in-store and online stock levels.

Manage all of your retail channels with ease. From brick-and-mortar locations to e-commerce platforms, our software helps you track sales, restock popular items, and maintain accurate stock levels across all stores and warehouses.

E-COMMERCE
Seamless integration with your online store.

Whether you sell on Shopify, WooCommerce, Amazon, or your own website, keep your inventory synced in real-time. Our platform ensures that stock levels across your sales channels are always accurate, reducing the risk of overselling and improving customer satisfaction.

MANUFACTURING
Track raw materials and finished goods effortlessly.

Manage every step of the production process with precision. Track the movement of raw materials and monitor the status of work-in-progress (WIP) items to ensure that your supply chain operates smoothly and efficiently.

DISTRIBUTION
Simplify complex supply chains.

Manage inventory across multiple warehouses and distribution centers. Our software gives you the tools to ensure products are delivered to the right locations, at the right time, with full visibility from end to end.
